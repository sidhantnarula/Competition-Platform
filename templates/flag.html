Level 1 - MNIST

Objective

This challenge involves identifying which models are backdoored in the MNIST dataset. You will work with four models: ResNet18, ResNet50, VGG16, and DenseNet121. Each model might be backdoored with one of four techniques: badnets, checkerboard, four-corner checkerboard, or random.

Introduction

In this challenge, you'll learn to identify backdoored models by analyzing their behavior and performance on the MNIST dataset. You'll download the dataset, the models, and perform analysis using provided tools.
